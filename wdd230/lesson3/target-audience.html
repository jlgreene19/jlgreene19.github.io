Target Audience

Description

  • Who: World travelers, Stay at home moms/dads, The elderly and/or retired community who make scheduled, weekly, monthly, bi-monthly trips to run errands, Gardners, Hobbiests who enjoy outdoor activities, such as hiking, camping, and beach trips.
  • Ages: 6-105
  • Technology: Any device capable of displaying a web browser: mobile phone, desktop, laptop, tablet, modern gaming system. Anyone capable of using any one of these devices.
  • Income: Given that the price range of these devices can have a large range, anyone of low, medium, or high income can benefit from the website. Income can be anywhere from $100,000 annually and below.
  • Motivation: People who want to plan their wardrobe depending on the weather, plan what they're going to do next weekend depending on if it's going to be hot or not, considering some spring cleaning if it's going to be raining over the next couple days, planning upcoming travel destination around the weather.
