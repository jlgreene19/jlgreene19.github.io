Overview

Purpose

The purpose of this website is for people to come and get the weather anytime, day or night. Maybe someone wants to plan what they're going to wear tomorrow depending on the temperature, or what they're going to do this weekend if it's going to be hot or not. If it's going to be raining for the next couple of days this would be the perfect time to do a little spring cleaning. This site will make a great source for someone wanting to plan their next few days around what the weather's going to be doing.
