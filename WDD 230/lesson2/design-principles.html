Design Principles

Jeremy Greene

White Space and Clean Design

Playstation

playstation.com/en-us/ps5

White Space: This website uses a lot of white space throughout the whole site to convey its message well. It uses dark images and text to contrast with the white background very well so there's no trouble seeing what they're trying to show the audience.

PARC: Alignment

eBay

www.ebay.com

Alignment: This website uses good alignment throughout the whole site. Where there are buttons they are aligned horizontally, text areas are justified to the left, and quick-view images for previously viewed auctions are also aligned horizontally.

Rule of Thirds

NFL

wwwl.nfl.com

Rule of Thirds: The photo at the top of the page uses rule of thirds. The football player is lined up on the right side of the image and the player graphic on the left is horizontally aligned with the bottom of the football player's chin.
